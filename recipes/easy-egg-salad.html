Easy Egg Salad

Great for lunch. Easy to make. Try on wheat bread with lettuce and tomato!

Easy, convenient, comforting, versatile, this egg salad recipe is a winner whatever the occassion

Ingredients

  • 8 hard boiled eggs, chopped
  • 1/4 cup plain, fat free yoghurt
  • 1 tablespoon parsley flakes
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. mix all ingrdients together
